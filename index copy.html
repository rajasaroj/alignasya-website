Signature modalities

Modern technique, thoughtfully applied.

Three certified modalities form the backbone of our hands-on care — each one chosen, never prescribed by default.

Trigger-point release through precision needling.

Sterile filament needles inserted into myofascial trigger points — a clinical, evidence-based way to release deep muscular tension that manual therapy alone can't reach.

  • Releases stubborn knots and chronic tension patterns
  • Targets sciatica, neck pain, frozen shoulder, plantar fasciitis
  • Single-use sterile needles, fully certified protocol
  • Visible relief usually within 24-48 hours

M.01 / DRY NEEDLING

Movement support without restriction.

Elastic therapeutic tape applied along muscles and joints — supports movement, decreases inflammation, and lets you stay active through recovery instead of immobile.

  • Reduces swelling and lymphatic congestion
  • Supports posture and movement patterns
  • Hypoallergenic, water-resistant — wear up to 5 days
  • Used widely by Olympic and pro-level athletes

M.02 / KINESIO TAPING

Ancient method, modern evidence.

Suction cups draw blood flow to specific regions — releasing deep fascia, breaking adhesions, and easing chronic muscular pain. Particularly effective for back, neck, and IT-band tension.

  • Releases fascial restrictions and adhesions
  • Improves localized blood flow and tissue oxygenation
  • Especially helpful for chronic back & shoulder pain
  • Combines beautifully with dry needling sessions

The skill of hands on tissue.
